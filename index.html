The Proposal



Previous Next



We got engaged on our recent trip to Tokyo, Japan on March 31st, 2015. While visiting the beautiful Chidorigafuchi Park, full of cherry blossoms and falling blossoms in the river, we rented a small row boat. After rowing out to the middle of river, John surprised Allison with a ring and proposed. Fortunately for him, Allison accepted the proposal!

Airports



Sonoma County Airport (STS)

2200 Airport Boulevard
Santa Rosa, CA 95403

The Sonoma County Airport is a tiny airport that is closest to Healdsburg. Alaska Airlines flys a limited number of direct flights connecting Sonoma County to Los Angeles, Orange County, Portland, San Diego and Seattle. Details on flight schedules are here



San Francisco Airport (SFO)

San Francisco, CA 94128

SFO is the largest international airport in the area. It is located about 1 hour, 30 minutes drive south of Healdsburg, CA.



Oakland Airport (OAK)

1 Airport Drive
Oakland, CA 94621

Oakland airport is another major airport in the bay area with most major airlines. It is located about 1 hour, 30 minutes drive south of Healdsburg, CA.
